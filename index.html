How to play: Use your arrow keys and WASD, or swipe with one and two fingers, to move the tiles in the 3rd to 6th dimensions. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

Note: 1×1×2×2×2×3 6-dimensional version created by Haihao Liu, strongly based on Huon Wilson's 4-dimensional one. You can play this on your phone via http://git.io/20486D. Beware, the controls are intentionally very counterintuitive!

Original created by Gabriele Cirulli. Based on 1024 by Veewo Studio and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer.
